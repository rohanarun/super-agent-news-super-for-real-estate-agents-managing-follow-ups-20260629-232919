Why computer‑use agents matter for real estate now

Industry analysis shows that a large share of real estate work — lead follow‑ups, routine client communication, listing coordination — is automatable with current agentic systems, not future tech.

Source: techandrealestate.com

Text assistants stop at advice

As the Coasty analysis explains, there is a sharp difference between text‑only assistants and computer‑use agents that can log into MLS systems, enter data into CRMs, send emails, and update schedules directly.

Once agents can operate computers, attackers adapt quickly. Intentional design, scope control, and repeatable workflows matter more than novelty demos.
